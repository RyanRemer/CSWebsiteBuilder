Introduction

For this assignment you will create a wireframe diagram and screenflow diagram in Lucidchart for the website you designed last week. Read the instructions and requirements below and have the assignment completed by the end of class.

Instructions

  1. Use your sketches from part 1 of this assignment to design a wireframe diagram in Lucidchart.

  1. Use your sketches from part 1 of this assignment to design a screenflow diagram in Lucidchart.

  1. Submit both diagrams as PDFs to learning suite.

Requirements
