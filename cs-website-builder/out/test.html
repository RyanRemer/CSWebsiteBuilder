Lab: Mr. Music Shop (Part 1)

UI Design Basics

Introduction

For this assignment, you will be sketching the design for a business called “Mr. Music Shop.” This business wants a website that allows them to sell musical instruments. Read the instructions below, then complete the assignment by the end of class.

Instructions

  1. Sketch a website that both meets the requirements below and that uses good UI design.
  2. Write 1-2 sentences on your Sketch about how your design follows each of the following principles.
    1. Contrast
    2. Repetition
    3. Alignment
    4. Proximity
  1. Hand in your sketch to the TAs before you leave class.

Requirements

  • The website has the following:
    • A home page with information on “Mr. Music Shop”
    • A page for browsing different musical instruments with user inputs for selecting items they want to buy (check boxes, buttons, etc)
    • One or more pages for purchasing desired items with one or more forms to fill out to provide purchase and shipping information
    • A contact page with business information (business location, phone number, etc)
  • The design shows the following:
    • Contrast
    • Repetition
    • Alignment
    • Proximity
